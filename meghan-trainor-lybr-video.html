Meghan Trainor unveils 'Let You Be Right' music video

Publish date: 2024-04-26

Meghan Trainor has kickstarted the week in the best way possible, by unveiling her dreamy, disco-inspired music video for ‘Let You Be Right’.

Joined by a group of dancers, the video for ‘Let You Be Right’ sees Meghan serve some of her best dance moves underneath a shiny disco ball. Shots of their fierce routine are intertwined with psychedelic effects, both of which complement the track’s throwback sound perfectly,

Check out the slick visual for ‘Let You Be Right’, directed by Colin Tilley, below.

‘Let You Be Right’ is taken from Meghan’s forthcoming third album, which is due for release soon. The album will also include the anthemic ‘No Excuses’ and the fierce bop ‘Can’t Dance’.

Whilst ‘Let You Be Right’ has already been building a steady online and streaming buzz, the release of the video is bound to give the song the boost it deserves to become one of the summer’s hottest tracks. Having officially impacted radio today, Meghan will also be performing the song on The Late Show with Stephen Colbert on CBS on June 6.

Alongside working on new music, Meghan will be returning as a panellist on FOX’s The Four: Battle For Stardom, which returns for its second season on June 7.
