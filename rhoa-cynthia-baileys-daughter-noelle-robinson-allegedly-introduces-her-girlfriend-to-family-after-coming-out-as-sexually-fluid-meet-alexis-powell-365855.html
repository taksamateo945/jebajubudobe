RHOA: Cynthia Baileys Daughter Noelle Robinson Allegedly Introduces Her Girlfriend To Family Afte

Publish date: 2024-01-30

Noelle Robinson’s coming out story was a beautiful one that was displayed earlier this season on Real Housewives of Atlanta. Now the kid that viewers watched grow up before their eyes has allegedly introduced her first girlfriend to her family.

Aceshowbiz shared a screenshot of Alexis Powell cozying up to the RHOA star’s daughter. They reported that Noelle was at a family dinner with the Howard graduate and Cynthia, Mike Hill, and her soon-to-be stepsisters.

This comes after Robinson told her mom that she also likes girls on the show. Not only did Bailey play it cool when she was hit with the shocking confession but she has made sure that her daughter knows she has her full support.

The supermodel opened up about the episode and her daughter’s sexuality on People TV.

‘I’ve always tried to be the kind of parent to Noelle to make her feel like she can tell me anything. I didn’t talk about sex [with] my mom, let alone my sexuality or anything like that. So I really wanted to make sure that she felt comfortable talking to me about it. And she did, as you saw.’

She went on to say how important it was that Noelle feels accepted.
