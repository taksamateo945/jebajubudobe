Pamela Anderson And Jon Peters Separate Only 12 Days After Their Surprise Wedding!

Publish date: 2024-07-26

It’s been not even two weeks since Pamela Anderson and Jon Peters tied the knot but it seems like they are already getting a divorce! The actress took to social media to share the news with her followers and ask them for ‘support.’

They may have been friends for many, many years but when it came to their marriage, it only lasted 12 days!

In a statement for THR, Pamela said: ‘I have been moved by your warm reception to Jon and I’s union. We’d be very grateful for your support as we take time apart to reevaluate what we want from life and from one another. Life’s a journey and love’s a process. With that universal truth in mind, we’ve mutually decided to put off the formalization of our marriage certificate and to put our faith in the process. Thank you for respecting our privacy.’

Furthermore, one source also tells HollywoodLife that the actress ‘asked for a break to think about this a little more.’

As you probably know, this shocking piece of information comes after it was confirmed that they had tied the knot in a small and intimate Malibu ceremony on January 20.

Amongst the few guests, her sons with ex Tommy Lee, Brandon, and Dylan, were in attendance, as well as Jon’s daughters and his former wife!

Their wedding seemed really sudden but this is not the first time they took their relationship a step further since they also dated in the 1980s.

After they broke up, however they remained friends and even live close by in the Malibu area.

It’s sad to hear that after only 12 days, they have decided to end things.
