Edinson Cavani caught swearing at fans after Man United lose against Crystal Palace

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Edinson Cavani has been filmed swearing at fans as he got onto Manchester United’s team bus following their 1-0 defeat against Crystal Palace.

United’s dismal campaign ended with a 12th league loss of the season as Wilfried Zaha scored the only goal of the game eight minutes before half time.

With United having finished down in sixth place, Cavani was clearly unhappy as he walked towards the bus, and he reacted to some fans taunting him by sticking his middle finger up at them. 

Edinson Cavani was unable to prevent Man United from losing against Crystal Palace

Wilfried Zaha scored the only goal of the game at Selhurst Park on Sunday afternoon

When he got onto the bus, he turned back again towards the crowd of people and repeated the action. 

It is unclear as to whether the supporters were Palace or United fans.

Earlier, Cavani made just his fifth start of the calendar year, but he was unable to make a telling contribution at Selhurst Park. 

Indeed, the 35-year-old missed a number of good chances which proved costly for the Red Devils. 

Cavani is expected to leave United when his contract expires next month

The Uruguayan striker has struggled with multiple fitness issues this season, which has led to him featuring in only 15 top flight matches.

He has only scored twice in 2021-22, some way down on the 17 that he managed in his maiden campaign at the club.

Cavani is set to depart United this summer when his contract expires at the end of June. 

His Old Trafford career looks to have ended on a sour note after the gesture he directed at supporters.
