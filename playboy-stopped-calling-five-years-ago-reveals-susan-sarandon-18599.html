Playboy Stopped Calling Five Years Ago: Reveals Susan Sarandon

Publish date: 2024-07-20

A lot of secrets were shared on Bravo executive Andy Cohen’s “Watch What Happens Live” yesterday when actress Susan Sarandon paid a visit to the show. But the one we loved the most? That Playboy magazine asked Sarandon to pose nude every year until she was 62!

"They haven't asked me in about five years. They used to ask me on a regular basis," the actress revealed. "I wanted to do it pregnant but they weren't game. That was before everyone else started doing it pregnant."

So would the Oscar winner ever consider it again? "I have sons now [so] I can't really [do it.]" Sarandon said. "When Lincoln Center did a thing and they showed a lot of my [old clips], my sons said, 'Didn't it ever occur to you that you would have children?' Then I asked my youngest, 'Are you okay?' And he said, 'Yeah, but scared, Mom.'"

Sarandon also revealed to Cohen that she was stoned at almost every single awards show she’s been to. When he originally asked her to name just one, she asked, “Only one?” and then went on to answer, “I would say almost all except the Oscars.”
