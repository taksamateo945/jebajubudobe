Kendall Jenner Walks the Runway at Chanel's Airport-Themed Show: Pics

Publish date: 2024-02-11

Mile-high style! Chanel held its Spring 2016 presentation on Tuesday, Oct. 6, and per usual, the beloved French fashion house did not disappoint with its theme: airport style.

The models, including Kendall Jenner, a favorite of designer Karl Lagerfeld‘s, strolled through Terminal No. 5 (same as Chanel’s famed perfume) clad in tweed and plaid perfection.

And of course, where there’s Jenner, there’s also her momager, Kris Jenner! The Kardashian-Jenner matriarch attended the show (alongside boyfriend Corey Gamble) to support her second-youngest child. Kris shared four photos from the event, including one that featured a Chanel Airlines sign on top of a bouquet of roses, which she captioned, “Getting ready to fly off to the Chanel Show to watch @kendalljenner on the runway!!! Good thing I was a flight attendant!”

The rising supermodel, 19, also had the support of her BFF Cara Delevingne, who sat in the front row next to girlfriend St. Vincent, and smooched Jenner on her cheek when she made a pit stop right next to her seat.

Delevingne shared the front row with more Lagerfeld favorites, including Johnny Depp‘s daughter, Lily-Rose Depp, her mother, longtime Chanel model Vanessa Paradis, The Maze Runner‘s Kaya Scodelario, and pop singer Janelle Monae, who rocked a fierce bouffant updo accented by a bedazzled, double-C pin.
