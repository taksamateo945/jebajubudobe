Love Island's Gemma Owen makes call over 'age gap' as viewers draw harsh comparison to dad Michael O

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Love Island viewers piled in on Gemma Owen during tonight’s episode as she and Davide discussed their age gap relationship – which led to fans bringing her famous dad Michael Owen into the conversation.

Gemma, 19, is the daughter of England and Liverpool legend Michael Owen. On Tuesday the teen chose to recouple with Italian hunk Davide, 27 – but many viewers were none too happy about the age gap relationship with some even threatening to call Ofcom.

And in Thursday night’s episode viewers saw Gemma and Davide discussing their fledgling relationship – and that eight year age gap.

“Your age is not an issue for me, is it an issue for you?” Davide told Gemma before adding: “You’re more mature than me.”

And the pair decided after the age gap discussion that neither was fazed by it.

Love Island viewers definitely were fazed, though.

@lukefuller said: “Davide how is it not an issue brev”

@ber1thax said: “The fact that the age gap isn’t a issue between these two is very very worrying”

@DntMakeaScene said: “Is it an issue for you? Big man you should be asking yourself this question”

@_Switch said: “Lmaooo Gemma trying the pull-back…. Davide suddenly concerned about age…”

@Tegaophelia said: “So they’re talking about age…and this is the outcome??”

@Yerdarj said: “HES CONFIRMED HE KNOWS HER AGE”

And others were concerned that Gemma told Davide she speaks to ‘much older’ men.

@meeshelles said: “Who are these “much older” men that Gemma is supposedly seeing????? Her dad doesn’t fight them??”

Some viewers also drew comparisons between Gemma Owen and her commentator dad Michael.

@Priskilla1807 said: “Michael Owen – dry pundit & commentator. His daughter Gemma defo takes after him”

@PassionatePogba said: “Michael Owen watching Gemma say she’s been with guys as old as him”

@Bioparlartee said: “Michael Owen watching that his daughter go for older men”

@Randyxmax said :”Who would have thought that Michael Owen’s daughter would be so unlikable”

@CFCangelia added, rather harshly: “shock of the year is finding out that michael owen is somehow not the most irritating person in his family”
