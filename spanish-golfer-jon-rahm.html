At age 25, Jon Rahm has many golfing accolades reaching a newer height and milestone. Besides the newly married has a supporting wife by his side who has already a special place in his heart and family.

Spanish Professional Golfer Jon Rahm plays US Open and PGA Tours. For both consecutive years in 2015 and 2016, he became the recipient of the Ben Hogan Award in 2015 and 2016, becoming the first player to win it twice.

Jon Rahm Bio & Family; Height, Parents, Wiki

The athlete was born as Rahm Rodríguez on 10 November 1994 in Barrika, Basque Country to parents, Edorta Rahm and Angela Rodríguez. He has got immense family support with some of his achievements like the 2015 Mark H. McCormack Medal per wiki.

Talking about his collegiate career, he closed the same career by winning the Pac-12 Conference championship and an NCAA regional championship before finishing tied for third in the national championship. In the 2016 US Open, he was the low amateur at the 2016 U.S. Jon stands at a height of 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 m) tall and weighs around 100 kg.

Career Details; Net Worth & Accolades

The 25-year-old net worth is $13 million. His professional career commenced in 2016 and in the same following year, he gained enough points as a non-member to earn a PGA Tour card for 2017. The same following year, he became the recipient of the Farmers Insurance Open. Some of his other achievements in 2017 are securing the 27th position in his first Masters including the first European Tour victory by winning the Dubai Duty-Free Irish Open by six strokes.

Moreover, in the 2018 season, he won the Open de España on the European Tour and Hero World Challenge tournament in the Bahamas. With so much of hard work and persistence, he became able to win the Zurich Classic of New Orleans on the PGA Tour with partner Ryan Palmer on 28 April 2019. Almost two months later, he finished tied for 3rd in the U.S. Open at Pebble Beach Golf Links in Pebble Beach, California on 16 June 2019. The year 2020 is also filled with lots of dreams and achievements for Job since he has won the Memorial Tournament, WGC-FedEx St. Jude Invitational, and BMW Championship.

Does Jon Rahm Have a Wife?

Jon has a wife and her name is Kelley Cahill. She was his longtime college girlfriend to whom he got engaged after dating for quite some time. The athlete’s nickname is Rambo and with his wife, they are yet to start a family.
