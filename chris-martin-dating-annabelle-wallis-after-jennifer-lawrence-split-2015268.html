Chris Martin Dating Annabelle Wallis After Jennifer Lawrence Split

Publish date: 2024-01-28

Onward, then. Less than two months after Us Weekly first reported that Chris Martin and Jennifer Lawrence had called it quits on their relationship for good, it appears that both parties have moved on.

Martin, 38, was recently spotted out on a romantic dinner date at Lucali in Brooklyn with Peaky Blinders actress Annabelle Wallis, and a source tells Us that the pair were “very clearly a couple.”

According to the eyewitness, Martin and Wallis, 30, were gazing into each others’ eyes and talking intimately, and the Coldplay frontman even had his arm around her.

“He has moved on quickly and has been seeing Annabelle Wallis for about a month,” another source told Us. “He visited her while she was filming in Vancouver and really likes her.”

Earlier this summer, Us Weekly exclusively reported that Martin and Lawrence, 25, had called it quits for the second time in the ten months that they had been dating. 

“Jen has been working nonstop and they never saw each other,” an insider told Us. The pair previously split last winter for about a month before getting back together in December.
