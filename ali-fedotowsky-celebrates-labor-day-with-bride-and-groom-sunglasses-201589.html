Ali Fedotowsky Celebrates Labor Day With Bride and Groom Sunglasses

Publish date: 2024-06-23

The future Mr. and Mrs. Manno! Ali Fedotowsky continued her engagement celebration while attending a Labor Day BBQ over the weekend. The Bachelorette alum and her husband-to-be, Kevin Manno, rocked “Bride” and “Groom” sunglasses while catching some rays outside.

“#TheFutureMannos #LaborDay2015 @kevinmanno,” Fedotowsky, 30, captioned the selfie. Clad in their bathing suits and tank tops, the couple also made sure to show off their red, white, and blue pride.

Manno took to his own account to share a different photo, of himself rocking a gold grill. He wore the sparkly teeth accessory while (what else!) grilling vegetable kabobs.
