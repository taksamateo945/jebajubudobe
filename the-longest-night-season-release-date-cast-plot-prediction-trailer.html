The Longest Night Season 2: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Trailer And More Update!

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Spanish content has been doing insanely great from the worldwide acclaimed series Money Heist and the Who Killed Sara thriller which took the world by storm. Money heist received its spinoff Berlin, but we are yet to be informed when the third season of Who Killed Sara will return on our screens. As we wait for the screen favorites, Netflix released another fan’s favorite Spanish series, The Longest Night. Created by Dose Morais, the series offers all the twists and suspense that fans who enjoy prison escape series like Prison Break would appreciate. Here is everything you need to know about the Netflix thriller series.

The Longest Night Season 2 Release Date: When will it be out?

So far, there is no announcement regarding the future of the thriller series. The crime series was released on 8th July this year and up until now, the show creators and Netflix is yet to give an update. The show has garnered a rating of 5.7/10 on IMDB and since series with the same ratings have gotten a renewal on IMDB, chances are that we may get a second season of the show.

The first season had six episodes and the second season might have the same number of episodes or more. If Netflix gives the green light for the second installment, then 2023 might be a good year for the show’s fans.

The Longest Night Season 2 Cast: Who will be in?

The series feature Alberto Amman (Narcos: Mexico) as Hugo Roca, Luis Vallejo as Simon Lago, Jose Luis Garcia (El Cid) as Lennon, Maria Caballero (The Girl in The Mirror) as Laura, Xabier Deive as Bastos AND Barbara Geoneag as Elisa Montero. Orther casts include Cecilia Freire as Manuela, Adolfo Fernandez as Dr Espada Laia Manzanares, Roberto Alamo and Daniel Albaladejo. If the series gets renewed for the second season, then we should expect all of the cast to reprise their roles apart from Sable Aran as Macarena Montes is likely to have died. We should also expect to see new faces as the story’s plot thickens.

A series trailer will not be available until the series gets renewed and the release date of the second season nears.
