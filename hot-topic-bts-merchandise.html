Hot Topic To Begin Carrying BTS Merchandise

Publish date: 2024-06-03

BTS fans in the United States and Canada are in for a special treat because American retailer Hot Topic has announced that BTS merchandise will be “coming soon” to its stores.

According to Billboard, the merchandise will consist of the official souvenir T-shirt from BTS’s Wings Tour, which began in February of last year and ended in December. The shirts will be available for purchase at 700 Hot Topic stores throughout the U.S. and Canada, as well as online. The retailer has yet to announce an exact date when the shirts will be available.

Hot Topic first teased the collaboration with BTS earlier this week, when its Instagram account began posting images spelling out “BTS,” as well as referencing the group’s songs, “No More Dream” and “Not Today.”

Following the announcement, fans revealed they have been requesting BTS merchandise in their local Hot Topic stores since last year.
